Gangadharbua Pimpalkhare "the guru" of his class

The Guru

He was born in marathi middle class family in 1910.He was principal or 'Mukhyapradhyapak' in corporation's school in Kothrud.He was disciple of legends like 'Vinayakrao Patwardhan' and Ramachandra Kundgolkar or 'Sawai Gandharva'. He adopted his unique style of teaching by blending Gwalior and Kirana Gharanas gayaki together.

A man of principle he was who always believed in simple living. He recieved the Pt.Vishnu Digambar Paluskar Sangeet Shikshak Gaurav Puraskar in 1998 given by Bharatiya Sangeet Prasarak Mandal, Puneâ€™s Gandharva Mahavidyalaya, Pune. He used to teach classical music relentlessly for more than 60 odd years. His musical day would start at 6:30 am in morning and would end at 8:30 pm in the evening. He was a Class in teaching by himself that's why his tuition class never wore a nameplate neither there was receipt book nor compulsion for fees .He groomed so many doyens in today's classical music like Vidushi Padma Talwalkar , Ranjana Pethe , Pandit Sanjeev Abhyankar , Rahul Deshpande, Amol Nisal, Pushkar Lele, Gauri Pathare & Dr Saleel Kulkarni to name a few.

Pandit Gangadharbuva Pimpalkhare puraskar is given to a deserving young classical music exponent and sangeet mehfil is organized by noted classical singer in month of August in his memory. This puraskar is instituted by Pandit Pimpalkhare bua Smruti Samiti uptil now Mrunmai Siknis/Pathak , Ramakanth Gayakwad, Suranjan Khandalkar, Atul Khandekar, etc have been awarded. Gandgadhar Swarotsav which is a 3 day musical program organised by Amol Nisal one of his disciples in his remembrance.

He moved on to heaven gates on 10th August 2003.
